### nba.lineups

- team_id integer REFERENCES nba.teams(team_id)
- player_ids integer[] -- Array of nba_id
- gp integer
- minutes numeric(8,2)
- off_rating numeric(5,2)
- def_rating numeric(5,2)
- net_rating numeric(5,2)
- ast_pct numeric(5,4)
- ast_tov numeric(5,2)
- ast_ratio numeric(5,2)
- oreb_pct numeric(5,4)
- dreb_pct numeric(5,4)
- reb_pct numeric(5,4)
- tm_tov_pct numeric(5,4)
- efg_pct numeric(5,4)
- ts_pct numeric(5,4)
- usg_pct numeric(5,4)
- pace numeric(6,2)
- pie numeric(5,4)

- created_at timestamptz
- updated_at timestamptz
- fetched_at timestamptz

#### Constraints & Indexes
- PRIMARY KEY (team_id, player_ids)
- INDEX ON (team_id)
- INDEX ON (player_ids)

#### Notes
- ⚠️ Deprecated: this table mixed season-level and game-level lineup concepts.
- Use:
  - `nba.lineup_stats_season` for `/season/lineups`
  - `nba.lineup_stats_game` for `/game/lineups`
- `player_ids` is a sorted array of official `nba_id`s representing the lineup.
