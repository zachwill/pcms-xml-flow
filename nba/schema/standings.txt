#### Notes
- This table tracks historical and current standings snapshots for teams.
- `standing_date` is the date part of the `time` field in the API's `StandingsMeta`.
- `season_year` is the starting year of the season (e.g., 2023 for the 2023-24 season).
- `season_label` is the display string for the season (e.g., "2023-24").
- `season_type` includes "Regular Season", "Pre Season", etc.
- Record columns (e.g., `home`, `road`, `vs_east`, `ahead_at_half`) are stored as text strings (e.g., "20-21") representing W-L.
- Clinch flags (`clinched_conference`, etc.) are converted from integers (0/1) to booleans.
- `playoff_seeding` may differ from `playoff_rank` due to play-in tournament outcomes or tiebreakers.
