### nba.tracking_stats

- game_id text REFERENCES nba.games(game_id)
- team_id integer REFERENCES nba.teams(team_id)
- nba_id integer REFERENCES nba.players(nba_id)
- minutes numeric(8,2)
- dist_miles numeric(6,2)
- dist_miles_off numeric(6,2)
- dist_miles_def numeric(6,2)
- avg_speed numeric(5,2)
- avg_speed_off numeric(5,2)
- avg_speed_def numeric(5,2)
- touches integer
- secondary_ast integer
- ft_ast integer
- passes integer
- ast integer
- cfgm integer
- cfga integer
- cfg_pct numeric(5,4)
- uf_fgm integer
- uf_fga integer
- uf_fg_pct numeric(5,4)
- fg_pct numeric(5,4)
- fg2m integer
- fg2a integer
- fg2_pct numeric(5,4)
- dfgm integer
- dfga integer
- dfg_pct numeric(5,4)

- created_at timestamptz
- updated_at timestamptz
- fetched_at timestamptz

#### Constraints & Indexes
- PRIMARY KEY (game_id, nba_id)
- INDEX ON (team_id)
- INDEX ON (nba_id)
- INDEX ON (game_id)

#### Notes
- `game_id`: Official 10-digit NBA game identifier.
- `team_id`: Team identifier.
- `nba_id`: Player identifier.
- `dist_miles`: Total distance traveled in miles.
- `dist_miles_off` / `dist_miles_def`: Distance traveled on offense and defense respectively.
- `avg_speed`: Average speed in miles per hour.
- `touches`: Number of times the player touched the ball.
- `secondary_ast`: Hockey assists (pass to the player who gets the assist).
- `ft_ast`: Passes that lead directly to a shooting foul and at least one made free throw.
- `passes`: Total number of passes.
- `cfgm` / `cfga`: Contested field goals made and attempted.
- `uf_fgm` / `uf_fga`: Uncontested field goals made and attempted.
- `dfgm` / `dfga`: Defended field goals made and attempted (at the rim/on defense).
- This table captures the high-level player tracking metrics provided by the tracking data processors (e.g., `TrackingBoxScore`, `PassBoxScore`).
- Data primarily derived from the NBA Tracking API endpoints or aggregated streams.
- All percentages are stored as `numeric(5,4)`.
