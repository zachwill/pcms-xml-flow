#### Constraints & Indexes
- PRIMARY KEY (game_id, team_id)
- INDEX ON (team_id)
- INDEX ON (game_id)

#### Notes
- `game_id` is the official 10-digit NBA game identifier.
- `team_id` is the official team identifier.
- This table stores team-level totals and team-specific metrics (e.g., `bench_points`, `points_in_the_paint`) that aren't purely aggregates of player stats.
- `minutes`: Total team minutes, typically 240 for a regulation game (48 * 5) or 240 + (5 * 5 * OTs).
- `bench_pts`: Points scored by the bench.
- `biggest_lead`: The largest lead held by the team at any point.
- `ast_tov_ratio`: Team-level ratio.
- `plus_minus`: For a team, this usually corresponds to the final margin if the game is over, but is tracked as a stat.
- `tov_team` vs `tov_total`: `tov_team` are turnovers not attributed to a specific player (e.g., shot clock violation). `tov_total` = sum of player turnovers + team turnovers.
- `reb_team`: Rebounds not attributed to a specific player (e.g., ball goes out of bounds after a missed shot).
